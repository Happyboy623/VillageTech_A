Our Uniqueness

Our commitment to fostering digital literacy and technical skills starts from an early age, instilling a love for technology and creativity in the young minds of the next generation. Through our innovative and engaging tech education programs, we aim to equip children with the tools they need to navigate the ever-evolving digital landscape confidently.

We understand that remote villages and township informal settlements often lack access to modern resources and opportunities. Therefore, we bring tech education directly to these communities, breaking down barriers and ensuring that no child is left behind. By leveraging the power of technology, we empower these young individuals to dream big and reach their full potential.

Our Success Stories

Five students from Mareetsane village had the remarkable opportunity to represent their community and Village Tech Academy at the Future Makers Hackathon. The event was hosted by the Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub in partnership with Telkom. The Hackathon provided an excellent platform for these students to showcase their tech skills and potential, while also gaining valuable experience. Their efforts and talents were recognized, as they emerged victorious, securing the first prize in the competition.

Keletso Motsikwe

Student

Collaborating with my teammates and brainstorming innovative ideas was a highlight of the event. Winning the hackathon was a thrilling achievement that inspired me to continue pursuing my passion for coding.
