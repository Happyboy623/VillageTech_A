Our Structured Courses

Each course is designed to be interactive and hands-on, incorporating practical projects and exercises to reinforce learning. Additionally, we provide mentorship and support to help students overcome challenges and nurture their passion for technology. Our goal is to equip the youth with the necessary skills to thrive in the digital world and contribute to the 4IR transformation.

Data Literacy & Visualization

Introduction to data and its significance in the 4IR & Data visualization tools
